# Impact on the Solana Ecosystem

By running a validator in Brazil, Pine Validator will:

- Increase SOL staking among Brazilian users.
- Onboard creators and users, driving growth and visibility for Solana.
- Enhance decentralization by expanding Solana's validator presence in an underrepresented region.

Brazil has immense potential to contribute to Solana's network, and Pine Validator is committed to making this a reality.
