# Validator Revenue Allocation

To ensure sustainable growth and maximum community impact, Pine Validator revenue (after covering server and vote costs) will be reinvested as follows:

A part of the resources is destined to onboarding users and educational initiatives, while the rest is reinvested into the validator (upgrades, stability & self-staking), staff compensation, and other operational expenses.

This transparent structure ensures the validator remains sustainable while actively contributing to the Solana community. Any future changes to this structure will be communicated in advance with stakers and supporters to maintain transparency and trust.
