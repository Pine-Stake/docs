# Introduction

Welcome to **Pine Stake** 🌲, a validator node on the Solana blockchain dedicated to decentralization, onboarding new users, and empowering the Brazilian creative and technical communities.

Brazil is the **4th largest country in global internet usage** (165.3M users), yet the Solana network has only one active public validator in the region. Pine Validator bridges this gap, offering education, accessibility, and a platform for Brazilian artists and stakers to thrive in the Solana ecosystem.

:::tip[Let's grow the pie together 🚀]

Pine Stake exists to make Solana staking accessible, educational, and rewarding for the Brazilian community.

:::
