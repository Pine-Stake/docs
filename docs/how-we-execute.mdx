# How We Will Execute Our Vision

## Education Initiatives

- Host Twitter Spaces, workshops, and community meetups to teach Brazilians about staking, NFTs, and the Solana ecosystem.
- Develop onboarding guides and tutorials for users and developers.

## Artist Support

- Provide technical assistance for minting and selling NFTs on Solana marketplaces.
- Give users their first SOL and NFT, empowering them to engage with Solana.
- Host hackathons and NFT showcases to highlight Brazilian artistic talent.

## Events

- Organize events such as hackathons, art exhibitions, and community showcases to drive awareness and adoption.
